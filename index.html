Who We Serve

Industries
We Supply

Our materials, services, and logistics expertise are purpose-built for the industries that form the backbone of American manufacturing and energy production.

01
Iron & Steel Mills
Our primary client sector. We supply scrap steel, steel byproducts, briquettes, and technical advisory to blast furnace and EAF/BOF operations. Former senior steel mill executives and metallurgical engineers on staff. Custom scrap blends to optimize output at the lowest cost per ton.
02
Wallboard Manufacturing
Drywall producers require consistent, reliable gypsum supply at specific quality grades. FeX sources domestically and internationally to ensure continuity. Off-spec washing capability provides a quality backstop when market supply tightens.
03
Cement Production
Cement plants require gypsum as a setting retarder, limestone as a primary feedstock, and mill scale as corrective material. FeX Group supplies all three, with delivery by truck, rail, or barge directly to your facility.
04
Agriculture
FGD gypsum is an increasingly important soil amendment for large-scale agriculture, improving soil structure and providing calcium and sulfur without significantly affecting pH. Both FGD and natural rock gypsum available in bulk.
05
Power Generation
Coal-fired power plants are both customers and supply partners. We source coal for plant operations and recover FGD gypsum — a high-value byproduct of the scrubbing process — from plant waste streams for distribution to other industries.
06
Foundry
Foundries require highly specialized scrap grades and byproduct materials. Our deep expertise in steel byproducts — blast furnace iron, tundish, pit scrap — and on-staff metallurgical engineers ensure material meets your exact furnace specifications.

Our Story

Trusted by Industry

Founded in 1998 with a clear purpose: bring genuine materials and metallurgical expertise into the brokerage space, and earn trust through knowledge — not just transactions.

From Regional Broker to Worldwide Sourcing Partner

FeX Group, LLC combines over 100 years of collective experience in the Iron and Steel Industry, operating both nationally and internationally. Founded in 1998, the company has grown from a regional materials brokerage into a worldwide commodities firm serving steel mills, foundries, power plants, cement producers, wallboard manufacturers, and agricultural operations globally.

Our workforce is what sets us apart. We've brought together former senior steel mill executives, operations managers, chemical engineers, metallurgical engineers, and process specialists — people who have spent careers inside the facilities we serve.

With the opening of our Mingo Junction processing facility in 2011, we expanded from pure brokerage into material handling, processing, and preparation. The facility receives materials by truck, rail, and barge.

Today, FeX Group operates across metals, minerals, recycling, and logistics simultaneously. Our gypsum division represents more than half of total business, with global port operations and ocean vessel logistics that connect suppliers and customers worldwide.
